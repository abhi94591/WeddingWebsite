Events

27th June

Mehndi 1PM - 5PM

It's one of the oldest traditional Indian pre wedding ceremony occur at bride's place where the bride will be the main part of the event and a professional mehndi artist applies mehndi to the bride's hands and feet. This will be a celebratory festival where people wearing vibrant clothes and dancing, singing, playing games and a lot more.

Cocktail Night 7PM - 12AM

This event is no way close to ethnic and will be fun event. The groom would like to have some shots before tying the knot with all of his friends. With booze flowing down the floor and the western attire on, everyone would be busy showing off their new moves on the stage which they have finely honed. Lastly, expect some beer pong matches & karaoke for sure.

28th June

Wedding 6PM-12AM

This is the main event in the entire wedding when the bride and the groom tie the knot. Many small traditional events will be happening on both groom's and bride's end. The groom comes with the barat and is greeted by the bride's family. Bride and groom will seek blessing from their relatives/friends attending the event. This is when the couple circle the sacred fire and are finally declared husband and wife.

29th June

Bride's homecoming 6AM-12AM

Here newly wedded bride will enter in her new home and will be welcomed by groom's family. From there bride and groom will attend few traditional ceremony events together with groom's family. After all this wedding is concluded, friends or relatives after having their meal will congratulate new couple and bid adieu.

Ugh... dress codes?

Mehndi 1PM - 5PM

Ethnic indian wear
Ladies can wear something bright & vivid and gentlemen can be on their vibrant kurtas with pants or pyjamas along with an optional shawl.

Cocktail Night 6PM - 12AM

Western attire
A semi-formal look that is contemporary & fashionable which is the best to pull off this night.

Wedding 6PM-12AM

Indo-western
Ladies can flaunt their unconventional sarees, lehengas, anarkalis, dresses and the list goes on whereas gentlemen can wear any kind of suit or just blazer. If you don't like to wear a suit/blazer, you can do a high-neck (band gala) waistcoat & shirt combo.
